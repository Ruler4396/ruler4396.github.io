### 3.2.1当$x$趋于定值
- $x→a$时，$f(x)→0$，$F(x)→0$
- 在$U(a)$内，$f'(x)F'(x)$存在且$F'(x)≠0$
- $lim_{x→a}\cfrac{f'(x)}{F'(x)}$存在或趋向于无穷大
则$lim_{x→a}\cfrac{f(x)}{F(x)}=lim_{x→a}\cfrac{f'(x)}{F'(x)}$

### 3.2.2 当$x$趋向无穷大

### #例子
1. 
$lim_{x→+∞}\cfrac{lnx}{x^n}$
$=lim_{x→+∞}\cfrac{\cfrac{1}{x}}{nx^{n-1}}=lim_{x→+∞}\cfrac{1}{nx^n}=0$

2. 
$lim_{x→1}\cfrac{x^3-3x+2}{x^3-x^2-x+1}$
$=lim_{x→1}\cfrac{3x^2-3}{3x^2-2x-1}=lim_{x→1}\cfrac{6x}{6x-2}=\cfrac{3}{2}$
