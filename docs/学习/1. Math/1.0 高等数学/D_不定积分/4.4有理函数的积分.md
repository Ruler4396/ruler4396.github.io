有理分式$\cfrac{P(x)}{Q(x)}$，其中$P(x)、Q(x)$均为多项式，次数分别为$m、n$


#多项式除法 
>分母减去分子与$nx$的乘积后的余数


### #例子 
1. #多项式除法 
$\cfrac{x^3+x^2-x+1}{x^2+x+1}$
$=x+\cfrac{-2x+1}{x^2+x+1}$

2. 
$∫\cfrac{1}{2x+1}$
$=\cfrac{1}{2}∫\cfrac{1}{2x+1}d(2x+1)=ln|2x+1|+C=ln|x-2|-ln|x-1|+C$

3. 
$∫\cfrac{1}{x^2-3x+2}dx$
$=∫\cfrac{1}{(x-1)(x-2)}dx=∫(\cfrac{1}{x-2}-\cfrac{1}{x-1})dx$
$=ln|x-2|-ln|x-1|+C$

4. 
$∫\cfrac{1}{x^2-2x+4}dx$
$=∫\cfrac{1}{(x-1)^2+3}d(x-1)=∫\cfrac{1}{√3}\cfrac{1}{(\cfrac{x-1}{√3})^2+1}d(\cfrac{x-1}{√3})$
$=∫\cfrac{1}{√3}arctan\cfrac{x-1}{√3}+C$

5. 
$∫\cfrac{1}{x^2-2x-3}dx$
$=∫\cfrac{1}{(x-1)^2-4}d(x-1)=∫\cfrac{1}{t^2-4}dt=∫\cfrac{1}{4}(\cfrac{1}{t-2}-\cfrac{1}{t+2})dt$
$=\cfrac{1}{4}ln|\cfrac{t-2}{t+2}|+C$

6. 
$∫\cfrac{x}{x^2+2x+2}$
$=\cfrac{1}{2}∫\cfrac{2x+2-2}{x^2+2x+2}dx=\cfrac{1}{2}∫\cfrac{d(x^2+2x+2)}{x^2+2x+2}-\cfrac{1}{2}∫\cfrac{2}{(x^2+2x+1)+1}d(x+1)$
$=\cfrac{1}{2}ln(x^2+2x+2)-arctan(x+1)+C$
