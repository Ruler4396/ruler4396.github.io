### 完全倒装 - 谓语动词完全在主语前
- **副词/介词在句首**
	- **地点副词** - here、there 强调地点
		- The last bus **goes**.
		- ==There goes the last bus.==
	- **时间副词** - now、then
		- ==Now comes the wolf‘s turn.==
		- 现在轮到大灰狼啦
	- **表运动方向的副词** - in、out、up、down
		- ==Up went the carrots in to the air.==
	- **[[介词短语]]**
		- ==**At the table** sat a rabbit.==
- **表语** - 形容词、分词、介词短语 &Such
	- ==**Seated on the gound** is a group of rabbits.==
	- 席地而坐的是一群兔子们
	- ==**Such** were the wolf's tricks.==
	- 这就是大灰狼的把戏

---
### 部分倒装 - 仅提前助动词
- 句首有否定意义词
	- ==*Never* before **have** I **eaten** such a delicious carrot.==
	- I have never eaten such a delicious carrot.
- **Only位于句首**
	- Only in this way **can** we grow delicious carrots.
	- Only then **did** the rabbit meet the wolf
- **If…should… [[虚拟语气？]]**
	- If I should win the lottery，I would buy a huge pile of carrots.(正常语序虚拟语气)
	- Should I win the lottery I would buy a huge pile of carrots.
- **固定句式**
	- Rabbits love eating carrots；so do wolves.
	- I was afraid.  So was I

---
### 形式倒装 - 谓语动词不提前，仅提前强调内容
- **[[感叹句]]**
	- What a delicious carrot it is.
- **比较级句型**
	- The more carrots you eat, the healthier you become.
	- If you eat more carrots, you will become heathier.(状语从句)
- **however、whatever引导的让步状语从句**
	- However long this video is, you should watch it till the end.
- **as、though引导让步状语从句(形式倒装)**
	- Much as he likes the carrot, he doesn’t want to eat it.
- **不知道是什么**
	- I did not eat a single carrot yesterday.
	- Not a single carrot did I eat yesterday.
