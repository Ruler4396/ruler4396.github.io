### 强调词
really 实在的
very 很
just 简直
ever 曾经
in the world/on world 

强调谓语动词 do/dose

强调代词 - 反身代词

### 举例
- I met a lovely rabbit in the field this morning.
- I **did** met a lovely rabbit in the field this moring.
- I met a **really** lovely rabbit in the field this moring.
- **What** a lovely rabbit I met in the field this moring.
- **How** lovely the rabbit I met in the field this morning was.
- **It was** this morning that I met a lovely rabbit in the field.
