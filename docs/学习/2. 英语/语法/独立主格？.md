### 1过去分词
- ***Because he was*** invited by the wolf, the rabit decided to go to the party. - 正常句子
- **Invited** by the wolf, **the rabbit** decided to go to the party. - **[[非谓语]]**
	- **主语the rabbit** **被邀请Invited**
---
### 2现在分词
- Because *the wolf* invited him, the rabbit decide to go to the party. - 正常句子
- *The wolf* **invited** him, the rabbit **decide** to go to the party. - 错误，一句话有两个**谓语**
- *The wolf* **inviting** him, the rabbit decided to go to the party. - 独立主格结构
	- *The wolf* 作为逻辑主语，是主动邀请的，**invited**用现在分词
---
### 3过去分词
- After the work was finished, the rabbit went home. - 一般句子
- The work finished, the rabbit went home. - 独立主格结构
---
### 4副词
- The rabbit is lying in bed and a carrot is still held in his hand. - 一般句子
- The rabbit is lying in bed, a carrot still held in his hand. - 独立主格
