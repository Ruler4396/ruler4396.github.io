### 与现在事实相反
- 如果我看见了兔子，我将去咬它
- If I see the rabbit **now**, I **will** bite him.(错误) - now 和 will时态冲突
后推一个时态
- If I saw the rabbit now, I would bite him.(正确) 
	- 条件句 动词变过去式
	- 主句 would+动词原形

---
### 与将来相反(大概率不可能发生)
- 万一我明天看见了兔子，我要去咬它一口
- If I should see the rabbit tomorrow, I would bite him.
	- 条件句 should(=were to，shall过去式)+动词原形
	- 主句 would +动词原形 (确定才用will bite)

---
### 与过去事实相反(如果过去怎么样)
- 如果几天前我看见了兔子，我就咬了它
- If I saw the rabbit a few days ago, I would bite him.(错误)
- If I had seen the rabbit a few days ago, I would have bitten him.
	- 条件句 had+动词过去分词
	- would have+动词过去分词

### 表愿望
- 单词
	- wish
	- would rather
	- **demand**
	- **suggest**
	- **insist**
	- **order**[^1][^2]
- **现在** wish+(that)+从句主语+动词过去式(be动词用were)
	- I wish (that) I were a rabbit
- **过去** wish+(that)+从句主语+had/would have+动词过去分词（过去完成时）
	- I wish (that) I had eaten the carrot.
- **将来** wish+(would/should/could/might)+动词原形
- 甚至**不需要**上面**这些**
	- It is (high[^3]) time that you ate a carrot.

---
[^1]:加粗部分一律用should+动词原形 *I order that you (should) give me a carrot.*，描述过去时用-ed
[^2]:从句形式 **宾从**：I suggest that you (should) eat a carrot.  **主从**[[从句]]：It is suggested that you (shoulg) eat a carrot.  **表从**：The suggestion is that you (should) eat a carrot.
[^3]:表示早就……
