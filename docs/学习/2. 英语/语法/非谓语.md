### 举例
- I am a rabbit, I eat carrots. - 错误句子
- I am a rabbit, so I eat carrots. - 并列句，两个句子
- Because I am a rabbit, I eat carrots. - 从句
---
### 不定式
- **To eat** a carrot every day is good for rabbit. - 不定式充当主语的一部分
- It is good for the rabbit to eat a carrot every day.
	- It is+形容词+for sb.+to do sth.
- It is good **that the rabbit eats a carrot every day**. - **主语从句**
---
### 动名词
- Carrots are healthy for the rabbit.
- **Eating carrots** is healthy for the rabbit.
